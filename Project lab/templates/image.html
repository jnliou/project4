Welcome to the Malaria Cell Identification Challenge!

The goal of this game is to assist individuals, especially medical and science students, in identifying malaria-infected cells.

How It Works:

  1. Click on the cell images that you believe show signs of malaria.
  2. Click to check if your answers are correct or not, and see what our Machine Learning model predicted.
  3. You can click to load a new set of images, allowing you to refine your identification skills.
